How to Make Pizza

Everyone loves a good pizza, right? It's a great way to feed a crowd and a perfect dish to bring to a potluck. It freezes well. It reheats well. Leftovers will keep you happy for days.

For this recipe, we are needing:

  • Pizza dough
  • 150g mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce

Follow these steps:

  1. Get it ready, open the dough and unwrapped it and pat it out and put the tomato sauce and the cheese on the dough.
  2. Put It in the Oven. set the oven on 475 degrees put the pizza in the oven and wait 10 to 15 min.
  3. Take it out.Cut it in slices and enjoy!
